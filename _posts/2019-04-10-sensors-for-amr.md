MiR 1000 sensors:
* laser scanners
* 3D cameras
* proximity sensors
* MiR AI Camera
* new proximity sensors to detect low-lying objects
* digital inputs and outputs
* cleaner e-stop functionality
* a new lighting scheme to help humans understand what the MiR1000 will do next.
